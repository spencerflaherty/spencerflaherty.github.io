## EV / Clean Energy (Blue Whale)

### Cooperative Purchasing Explainer

Produced a video explaining how public agencies can bypass traditional RFPs and procure EV charging through Blue Whale's Sourcewell contract—blending AI-generated visuals with traditional editing to keep it engaging while clearly communicating a complex procurement process.

**The Stack:**
- AI Video: Kling 2.6 (start/end frame + motion control)
- AI Image: Nano Banana
- Script: Claude
- Post-Production: DaVinci Resolve

### Automated ROI Report Funnel

Created a full-funnel ad campaign promoting a property-specific ROI report as a lead magnet—ad creative shows sample results, landing page captures survey responses via Google Form, and an MCP agent automatically generates a custom report that makes the value proposition impossible to ignore, improving quote-to-close rate.

**The Stack:**
- Ad Creative: DaVinci Resolve
- Landing Page: Custom Design + Copy
- Data Capture: Google Forms
- Report Automation: Claude Skill + MCP Agent

## Electrical Services (Critical Peake)

### $20 CPL Photo Campaign

Achieved $20 average CPL by shooting real technicians on real job sites, overlaying authentic Google reviews, and creating dozens of photo/copy variations to let Facebook's algorithm find the winners—all targeted to specific service areas via Conversions API.

**The Stack:**
- Platform: Facebook Ads
- Creative: Real Job Site Photography + Review Overlays
- Variation Testing: Dozens of Photo/Review/Style Combinations
- Targeting: Geo-Fenced Service Areas
- Optimization: Conversions API + Maximize Conversions

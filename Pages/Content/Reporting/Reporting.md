## Performance Measurement

### Custom Attribution System

Built a multi-touch attribution model connecting lead sources, sales activity, and online behavior to revenue—enabling smarter ad spend and rep accountability.

**The Stack:**
- BI Layer: Microsoft Power BI
- Custom Tooling: Vibe-Coded Dashboard
- Data Sources: CRM, Ad Platforms, Web Analytics

### Pipeline & Churn Dashboards

Built Power BI dashboards with automated alerts for pipeline health, churn risk, and sales activity—delivering insights via email, Slack, and in-app notifications.

**The Stack:**
- BI Layer: Microsoft Power BI
- Notifications: Email, Slack, In-Dashboard
- Data Joins: CRM + Sales Activity + Job History

## Customer Operations

### Automated Review Request Pipeline

Pulls recent customers from CRM, filters out existing reviewers, and sends AI-varied email requests for Google reviews. Increased both review volume and average rating.

**The Stack:**
- Data Source: SimPro (CRM) + Power BI
- Email: iContact
- AI Layer: Claude (RAG personalization)

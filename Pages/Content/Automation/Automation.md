## Outbound Lead Gen - any lead data you can incorporate?

### Geospatial Prospecting

Architected an end-to-end pipeline to identify high-value EV infrastructure leads by filtering physical land attributes, resolving ownership, and automating executive outreach.

**The Stack:**
- AI Logic: Agentic Workflows
- Spatial Data: OpenStreetMap
- Property Data: Public Land Records
- B2B Intelligence: ZoomInfo
- Execution Layer: Apollo

### Gov-Procurement Pipeline

Engineered a public-sector lead generation engine by scraping public procurement datasets, collecting agency persona contact info, and automating high-intent outbound sequences.

**The Stack:**
- Ingestion Layer: Scraper Agent
- Enrichment Layer: ZoomInfo
- Execution Layer: AI Logic + Apollo.io

### Arcadia Run Outreach Campaign

Automated personalized outreach to peer properties by pulling CRM history for each contact, generating tailored emails referencing past interactions, and embedding case study links—making each message feel hand-written while scaling across the mid-Atlantic.

**The Stack:**
- Data Source: CRM Customer History
- AI Logic: Dynamic Email Personalization
- Content Asset: Video Case Study
- Execution Layer: Automated Sequence Delivery

## Inbound Lead Gen + Management

### Intent Conversion Engine

Built an inbound funnel that turns anonymous website traffic into qualified leads by identifying visitors through IP-to-company matching, layering in third-party intent signals to gauge buying readiness, and auto-routing high-fit prospects to sales.

**The Stack:**
- Identity Resolution: Visitor De-anonymization (RB2B, Warmly, etc.)
- Intent Layer: Bidstream + Co-op Data (Bombora, Demandbase)
- Orchestration Layer: Zapier

### Paid Acquisition Engine - any optimization bs numbers you can pull in terms of time saved?

Designed a high speed-to-lead system by synchronizing Facebook and LinkedIn form submissions into CRM, routing validated leads via Zapier, and triggering instant rep notifications to maximize peak-intent conversion.

**The Stack:**
- Platforms: Facebook, LinkedIn
- Orchestration Layer: Zapier
- Notification Layer: Slack/Email/SMS
- Pipeline Integration: CRM
